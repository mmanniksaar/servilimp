Attachable Glass Holder

The Carat collection is based on a contemporary interpretation of the traditional glass grinding for which Orrefors is world-renowned. The irregular geometry produces beautiful reflections of light in the crystal. Carat Coupe is a glass for sparkling drinks and cocktails. The glass has a wide bowl that allows the drink to breathe, for a more developed aroma. It can also be used for desserts. The stem of the glass is covered with the collection’s characteristic pattern. Designed by Lena Bergström.
